Filter by result (fail/warn/pass) and cluster to narrow down. Failures are also available as individual Findings on the Findings page.

Improve coverage: deploy more policies in audit mode first, review violations here, then switch to enforce once workloads are clean — only deployed policies produce reports, so uninstalled ones won't appear.
